About Me

I am Anisa Chokwenda, born and bred in Zimbabwe. I stay in a town called Chegutu which is an hour drive from the Capital city, Harare. I have a Bachelors Degree in Chemical and Process Systems Engineering. Currently, studying at BYU has increased my faith in Jesus Christ. I'm fond of a lot of exciting things especially visiting historical sites in my country. I grew up playing baseball and softball. In the community, we grew up playing local games like mahumbwe (family and cooking), chamuhwande-hwande (hide and seek) and many more. I grew up having the pride of ubuntu, which is humanity that we respect our elders by greeting them showing courtesy and also helping them with chores. I love my culture and it has shaped me to be who I am now.
