Welcome to Food Culture Community

Purpose

The community serves to promote cultural exchange from around the world, to foster cross-cultural understanding, connections and cooking techniques.

We support sustainable food systems by advocating for sustainable food practices, hence reducing waste, improving health eating and the importnce of culturally acceptable and nutritious diets.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What features would be recommended so that people can share their recipes?
  • Scenario 2: How do people share their experiences and recipes so that they can receive their feedback?
  • Scenario 3: How can I manage food cravings and develop a healthier relationship with food?
